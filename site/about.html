About Paul

Short story

Paul Smith is a computer programmer and cofounder of EveryBlock. He lives in Baltimore, MD.

Long story

Paul was born on the eastern shore of Maryland, and grew up surrounded by farmland outside of Frederick, MD.

Paul moved to Chicago in the winter of 1999-2000. He worked at the Center for Neighborhood Technology, starting off as an intern working one of CNT’s websites, and from there became the systems administrator and webmaster.
